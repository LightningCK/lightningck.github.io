About

The human
behind the code

Hey — I'm Chinnapat Chaionkaew, a full-stack engineer with 7 years of professional experience building products people actually love to use. I grew up tinkering with hardware, which is probably why I care just as much about performance under the hood as I do about pixel-perfect UIs.

I studied Computer Science at UC Berkeley and spent the early years of my career at startups, where I learned to move fast without breaking things (okay, sometimes breaking things). These days I specialise in React ecosystems, Node.js backends, and cloud-native architecture on AWS and GCP.

When I'm not staring at a terminal, I'm hiking the Marin Headlands, reading sci-fi, or obsessing over my espresso extraction ratios. I believe that great software is just a series of well-solved, tiny problems.
